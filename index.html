About Me

I went to West Java in 2015 to pursue my bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering at Telkom University.
After graduating in 2019, I continued my studies and earned a master's degree in Control and Computer Systems from Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB).
I am deeply passionate about working on projects related to the ocean and underwater technology.
I have worked on designing attitude control systems for quadcopters and developing frameworks for obstacle detection and avoidance systems for underwater robots.
